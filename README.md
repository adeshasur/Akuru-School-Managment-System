## 🏗️ **Overview**

**Akuru** is a modern, comprehensive, and scalable School Management System designed to streamline educational workflows. From student enrollment to alumni management, Akuru centralizes all academic and administrative operations into a single, intuitive platform.

Whether you're an Admin, Teacher, Student, or Parent, **Akuru** provides specific portals tailored to your needs, ensuring transparency, efficiency, and real-time communication.

---

## ✨ **Key Features**

### 👨‍💼 **Administrative Portal**
- **Inventory Management**: Track school assets and supplies.
- **Notice Board**: Publish and manage important school-wide notifications.
- **Event Planning**: Schedule and organize school events and extracurriculars.
- **User Management**: Control system access for staff and students.

### 👩‍🏫 **Teacher Portal**
- **Classroom Management**: Assign and manage student groups.
- **Attendance Tracking**: Effortless daily attendance marking.
- **Exam & Results**: Generate exams and publish results instantly.
- **Home Assignments**: Manage and grade student submissions.

### 👨‍🎓 **Student Portal**
- **Personal Dashboard**: Track marks, attendance, and progress.
- **Fee Management**: View and process educational fees (Invoices).
- **Library Hub**: Borrow books and check resource availability.
- **Transport Tracking**: Real-time vehicle and route information.

### 👨‍👩‍👧 **Parent Portal**
- **Progress Tracking**: Monitor your child's academic journey.
- **Seamless Communication**: Get instant updates on attendance and disciplinary records.

## 🚀 **Technology Stack (Proposed)**

- **Frontend**: React.js / Next.js
- **Backend**: Node.js / Express
- **Database**: PostgreSQL / Supabase
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Authentication**: JWT / Auth.js
